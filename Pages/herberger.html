Looking for apartments closest to the Herberger Institute for Design and the Arts? Top 3 Closest Walkable Apartments for Herberger

Ranked for Herberger students who care most about getting to design and arts classes with the least hassle.

Rank #1

Atmosphere Tempe

707 S. Forest Ave • Directly near the Forest Mall / Gammage area

👣 4 mins View Website →

Atmosphere Tempe is one of the best overall options for Herberger students because of how directly it connects to the Forest Mall area. If your priority is minimizing your walk to class while living in a student-centered and amenity-heavy building, this is one of the strongest choices.

Why it stands out

  • One of the shortest walks to Herberger
  • Very direct route to the arts core
  • Student-focused and modern

Best for

Students who want a quick walk to class and a building with strong shared spaces and amenities.

What to look for

  • Check parking and extra monthly fees
  • Compare floor plans for quieter units
  • Ask about study lounge availability

Student vibe

Modern, social, and highly convenient for students with class-heavy schedules.

Rank #2

Union Tempe

712 S. Forest Ave • Downtown Tempe / direct access to Forest Mall

👣 4 mins View Website →

Union Tempe is another top-tier Herberger option because it sits right along the Forest corridor, making the walk to Gammage and nearby arts buildings extremely short. It is especially good for students who want to be close to both class and the downtown Tempe food and social scene.

Why it stands out

  • Extremely close to Herberger
  • Great downtown and campus balance
  • Strong everyday convenience

Best for

Students who want a lively environment and fast access to both campus and Mill Avenue activity.

What to look for

  • Noise levels depending on floor and unit
  • Parking and utility costs
  • Furnished versus unfurnished options

Student vibe

Urban, energetic, and one of the most convenient overall choices for Herberger students.

Rank #3

ōLiv Tempe

704 S. Myrtle Ave • Just west of Forest / strong access to Herberger

👣 5 mins View Website →

ōLiv Tempe rounds out the top three because it still gives Herberger students a strong walkable location while adding a more polished and premium-feeling apartment experience. It is a good fit for students who want quick campus access without giving up a more curated living setup.

Why it stands out

  • Strong walkability to Herberger
  • Premium student-living feel
  • Great balance of convenience and comfort

Best for

Students who want a polished apartment experience while staying close enough to class for a practical walk.

What to look for

  • Compare pricing with Union and Atmosphere
  • Check room size and layout options
  • Ask about rooftop and study amenities

Student vibe

Stylish, polished, and strong for students who want a more upscale living environment near campus.

Note: “Closest” here is meant for practical walking distance to the Herberger Institute area near ASU Gammage, not just being generally close to ASU Tempe.
