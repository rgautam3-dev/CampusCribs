Top 3 Closest Walkable Apartments for W. P. Carey

Ranked for W. P. Carey students who care most about getting to business classes with the least hassle.

Rank #1

Union Tempe

712 S. Forest Ave • Downtown Tempe / near Mill Ave

👣 11 mins View Website →

Union Tempe is the strongest first option for a W. P. Carey page because it combines a very central downtown location with easy access to ASU and a lifestyle that feels connected to both school and city life. If someone wants to be able to walk to business classes, grab food quickly, and stay near Mill Avenue activity, this is one of the best fits.

Why it stands out

  • Excellent overall location for W. P. Carey students
  • Close to campus + close to downtown Tempe
  • Good for students who want convenience and energy

Best for

Students who want a central, lively environment and want to be near restaurants, friends, class buildings, and social spots.

What to look for

  • Check whether your preferred unit is furnished or unfurnished
  • Compare parking cost if you plan to bring a car
  • Ask about noise levels depending on tower/floor

Student vibe

Urban, active, and social. A good choice if you want more than just a place to sleep.

Rank #2

Atmosphere Tempe

707 S. Forest Ave • Steps from Rusty Taco and Happy Joe

👣 12 mins View Website →

Atmosphere Tempe is another top-tier W. P. Carey option. It is very close to the same high-value zone as Union, but feels especially appealing for students who want a more explicitly student-oriented housing setup with built-in study and amenity spaces.

Why it stands out

  • Very strong location for walking to campus
  • Feels purpose-built for student routines
  • Good amenity mix for studying and hanging out

Best for

Students who want convenience, modern finishes, and building amenities that support both schoolwork and downtime.

Questions to ask

  • How crowded are the study lounges during peak exam weeks?
  • What floor plans are best for quieter living?
  • How much does parking add to the total monthly cost?

Student vibe

Modern, high-rise, student-centered, and amenity-heavy. Great if you want your building to support your school life.

Rank #3

ōLiv Tempe

704 S. Myrtle Ave • Further south toward Apache Blvd

👣 13 mins View Website →

ōLiv Tempe rounds out the top three because it still places you in a strong walkable zone for W. P. Carey, while also offering a polished student-living feel. It is a good middle ground for someone who wants a premium, community-style building but still values staying close to campus.

Why it stands out

  • Strong walkability to W. P. Carey
  • Good balance of convenience + student amenities
  • Feels polished and lifestyle-focused

Best for

Students who want a modern, furnished-feeling environment and a property that emphasizes design and shared spaces.

What to compare

  • Lease pricing versus Union and Atmosphere
  • Study-space quality and availability
  • How important garage parking and rooftop amenities are to you

Student vibe

Stylish, social, and convenience-driven. Good for students who want walkability without giving up the apartment experience.

Note: “Closest” here is meant for practical student walking to the W. P. Carey area, not just general “near ASU.” Routes can still vary depending on which building, entrance, and path you use.
